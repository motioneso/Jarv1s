Workflow Preferences: Decision

Do not make "Workflow preferences" a large default section. Most preferences belong with the module or product area they affect.

Where these settings should live
Tasks default view
Lives in Tasks settings or inline in Tasks. Core settings can link to Tasks settings.
Notification sensitivity
Lives in Notifications settings. Could be linked from a small "Attention & notifications" row.
Briefing cadence and time
Lives in Briefings settings. Core settings links to Briefings when enabled.
Calendar/email behavior
Lives in data-source settings and module settings, not a generic workflow page.
Only keep top-level if cross-Jarvis
Example: global quiet hours or timezone, if used across notifications, briefings, planning, and scheduling.
