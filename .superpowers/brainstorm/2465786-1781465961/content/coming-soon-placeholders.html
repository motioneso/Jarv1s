Placeholder Rule

Coming-soon settings can stay visible when they explain the product direction and help design the eventual surface. They should not look broken or like disabled production controls.

Use placeholders for future controls
Allowed placeholder shape
Future setting name, one-line purpose, "Coming soon" badge, optional learn-more/status text.
Avoid
Disabled switches with no explanation, fake controls, backend enum labels, or forms that cannot save.
Examples
Remember facts about me; notification sensitivity; briefing cadence; module tool permissions; assistant autonomy levels.
